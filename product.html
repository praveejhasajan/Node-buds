Our Products

At Nodes n Buds, we provide the finest quality products directly from landscapes to your doorstep. Whether you are a tea connoisseur or culinary enthusiast, we have something special for you.

Green & Black Tea

Green tea is widely promoted as a dietary supplement for weight loss and cardiovascular health. It is minimally processed and is distinguished by its lighter colour, subtle flavour, and higher antioxidant content. Experience the refreshing taste and numerous health benefits of our premium green teas.

Varieties we offer:

  • Green Orthodox
  • Speciality Green Tea
  • Flavoured Green Tea

Black tea undergoes an extensive oxidation process and is distinguished by its darker colour and stronger flavour. Indulge in the rich, robust taste of our black teas, perfect for any time of the day.

Varieties we offer:

  • Assam Black CTC Tea
  • Assam Black Orthodox Tea

Bamboo Shoot

Available in fresh and fermented form, bamboo shoots or bamboo sprouts are the edible shoots of many bamboo species. They are a powerhouse of vitamins and minerals, which helps in boosting the immunity. Add a unique, crunchy texture and a burst of nutrients to your meals with our bamboo shoots.

Varieties we offer:

  • Fresh (seasonal)
  • Fermented
  • Pickle

Assam Lemon

Locally known as Kaji Nemu, it is renowned for its size, distinct fragrance and antioxidant properties. It is much juicier than other varieties of lemons. Awarded the prestigious Geographical Indication (GI) status in 2019, it has been declared as the state fruit of Assam, India. Whether squeezed over seafood delicacies or infused into your favourite beverages, its vibrant flavour is sure to tantalize your taste buds. Bring home the essence of Assam with our premium lemons.

Citrus Maxima (Seasonal)

A native of Southeast Asia, it is known as Robab Tenga, Pomelo or the Shaddock Fruit. Being the largest citrus fruit, it is celebrated for its sweet yet tangy taste and luscious juiciness. It is highly nutritious, full of fiber and rich in antioxidants and is believed to contain several days’ worth of the recommended daily levels of vitamin C. Enjoy the refreshing and healthful benefits of our Citrus Maxima.

Turmeric

India is the largest producer of turmeric in the world. Used in both Ayurvedic and Chinese medicines, turmeric contains a powerful antioxidant called curcumin. Traditionally, it is recognised for its anti-inflammatory properties and is used in medicines to treat digestive and liver problems, skin diseases and wounds. Enhance your health and culinary creations with our high-quality turmeric.

Ginger

Native to Southeast Asia, ginger is widely used as a spice and herbal medicine. It has immune-boosting properties and contains a bioactive compound called gingerol, which is responsible for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Add a zesty kick and numerous health benefits to your dishes with our premium ginger.
